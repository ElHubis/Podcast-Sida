Webbpodden is a podcast that likes to discover and investigate the parts of the internet that are often hidden from the public view. Together with the Hosts Ruben and Ward we take a look at the darkweb and the deepweb, encryption and onion browsers. There our hosts find everything from illegal weapons deals and drug sales to the chatrooms of whistleblowers. Follow our hosts down this rabbit hole if the dark corners of the internet pique your interest
