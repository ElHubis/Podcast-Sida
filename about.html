About The Podcast

Webbpodden was started in 2022 by Ruben Mancos and Ward Dabbah. The podcast is about all things internet, (hence the name) but especially likes to look through and discuss the darker parts of the world wide web that connects us. The podcast began with our episode about the darkweb and represnts perfectly what our podcast is about, we find nothing more interesting than taking a peek at all things hidden and obscured. When we do this we don't just find what's out there, but also give out best efforts to find out how it works, all the way from the code to the economic systems. Even though the internet often can be a dark place there is almost always something good to find in those deep dark corners and we enjoy observing that just as much as the 'bad' part of the web. If you want to see what hidden things there are to be found in our digital world, you have come to the right place.

Webbpodden is a podcast that likes to discover and investigate the parts of the internet that are often hidden from the public view.
